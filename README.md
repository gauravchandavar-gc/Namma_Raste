# Namma Raste – Smart Road Safety & Traffic Management System

## Overview

Namma Raste is a smart road safety and traffic management platform designed to improve urban transportation efficiency, reduce accidents, and enhance public safety using modern technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, real-time monitoring, and intelligent analytics.

The project focuses on solving major transportation challenges including:
- Traffic congestion
- Delayed emergency response
- Road accident monitoring
- Unsafe driving conditions
- Poor road condition reporting
- Lack of real-time traffic insights

The system provides a scalable and user-friendly solution for smarter and safer roads in modern cities.

---

# Problem Statement

Urban areas are facing increasing traffic density, frequent road accidents, and inefficient traffic monitoring systems. Traditional systems are unable to dynamically adapt to changing road conditions and emergency situations.

Namma Raste addresses these issues by creating a centralized platform capable of:
- Monitoring road conditions
- Managing traffic efficiently
- Reporting accidents
- Providing live alerts
- Supporting smart-city infrastructure

---

# Proposed Solution

Namma Raste integrates intelligent monitoring and automation features to assist both citizens and authorities.

The system provides:
- Real-time road monitoring
- Smart traffic analysis
- Accident reporting and alerts
- Emergency support assistance
- Road issue detection
- User-friendly dashboard
- Data-driven traffic insights

This helps improve road safety, reduce response time during emergencies, and support better city traffic management.
